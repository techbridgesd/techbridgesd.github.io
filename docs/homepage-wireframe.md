## Notes
- **Hero content** sits bottom-left over a full-screen 3D background, with a dark
  overlay so text stays readable.
- **Color cues:** navy text/sections, teal accents and primary buttons, gold for
  the "SD" wordmark and highlight CTA.
- **Responsive:** the two-column Overview and four-column stats collapse to a
  single column on phones; the nav becomes a menu button below 1024px.
